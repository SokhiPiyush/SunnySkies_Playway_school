Learning Programs

Thoughtfully designed curricula that adapt to each child's developmental stage and learning style

🐣

Nursery (1.5-2.5 years)

Gentle introduction to group learning with sensory exploration, basic social skills, and nurturing care in a home-like environment.

🌸

Junior KG (2.5-3.5 years)

Foundation building through structured play, early language development, motor skills enhancement, and creative expression activities.

🚀

Senior KG (3.5-4.5 years)

School readiness preparation with phonics, numeracy, writing skills, critical thinking, and independence building activities.
